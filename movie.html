3/20/2024｜电影：最爱女人购物狂（The Shopaholics）

导演：韦家辉

主演：张柏芝、刘青云、陈小春、官恩娜、王天林、罗家英、夏春秋、邵美琪、郭蔼明、车婉婉、路斯明、陈逸宁、徐自赞、骆力炜


电影海报


简介：方芳芳是一个超级购物狂，这令她连番失业。方芳芳决定去看精神科医生，不想这个医生李简仁有选择恐惧症。看病过程中，方芳芳协助早产的护士生产，因此获得工作，但是要帮李简仁解决选择问题。 不久方芳芳遇上同样是购物狂的何穷富，方芳芳充当心理医生帮他治疗。没想到何穷富是上市集团的大老板，他把方芳芳的省钱方法用在生意上，令公司赚钱，同时也对这个女人产生了感情。与此同时，方芳芳同时答应了何穷富和李简仁二人的约会邀请。丁叮当与李简仁二人分手后患上失恋创伤后遗症，李简仁的选择恐惧症同样发生在感情事上。但四人性格上的缺点令他们不知道最爱是谁，因此他们同时进行四个婚礼，在婚礼的过程中认知到自己的缺点并找到了最爱。

Synopsis: Fang Fangfang is a super shopaholic, which has caused her to lose her job repeatedly. Fang Fangfang decided to see a psychiatrist, not wanting this doctor Li Jianren to have a phobia of choice. During the medical treatment, Fang Fangfang assisted the nurse who gave birth prematurely, so she got a job, but she had to help Li Jianren solve the problem of choice. Soon Fang Fangfang met He Poor Rich, who was also a shopaholic, and Fang Fangfang acted as a psychiatrist to help him treat. Unexpectedly, He Poor Fu was the big boss of the listed group, and he used Fang Fangfang's money-saving methods in business to make the company make money, and at the same time he also had feelings for this woman. At the same time, Fang Fangfang agreed to the date invitation of He Poor Fu and Li Jianren at the same time. Ding Dingdang and Li Jianren suffered from post-traumatic sequelae of lovelorn after they broke up, and Li Jianren's phobia of choice also occurred in emotional matters. But the shortcomings of the four personalities make them not know who their favorite is, so they have four weddings at the same time, and in the process of the wedding, they recognize their shortcomings and find the favorite.
